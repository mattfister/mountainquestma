Buckman Tavern

Lexington, MA

Overview

The Buckman Tavern is a historic site important in the Revolutionary War. Multiple hauntings have been reported here. These include a ghostly figure and footstep noises on the closed upper floor and a chair that changed positions when no one was in a room to move it.
