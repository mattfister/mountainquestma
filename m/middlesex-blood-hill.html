Mountain Quest Massachusetts

Blood Hill

Middlesex, MA

Date Hiked: 9/29/18

Description

Blood Hill is a small peak in North Western Middlesex County and one of its highest elevations. There is a short trail (approximately .75 miles) from a parking area at the end of Luke road. The trail was partially a stream at lower points. The peak itself is bald and had a good view to the North East.
